Kaospilot Masterclass - The Art and Craft of Facilitating Learning Spaces Masterclass
November 24, 2017 – November 26, 2017, 9 a.m. – 5 p.m.
The Art and Craft of Facilitating Learning Spaces Masterclass is a 3-day masterclass offered by Koaspilot in collaboration with the Centre for Teaching and Learning. Explore experiential ways of learning and imaginative processes for transforming curriculum with process consultant, David Jul.
