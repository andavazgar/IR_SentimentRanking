CSEP Workshop
Need practical help during the CSEP application process? Come to a workshop!
November 30, 2017, 3 p.m. – 4 p.m.
Attended an information session but now you're in need of some practical help? Not a problem, with our new weekly workshops! Stop by with a tablet or laptop and we can help you with any practical questions or issues you may be having with any step of the application process. You can also connect with other students completing their applications and get to know your fellow future CSEP participants!
