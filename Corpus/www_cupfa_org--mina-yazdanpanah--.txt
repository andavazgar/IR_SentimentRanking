Mina Yazdanpanah teaches Digital Signal Processing (ELEC6601) at Concordia University. She received her PhD from the Electrical & Computer Engineering department at Concordia University in 2012. Her research specialty is Advanced Techniques for Optimizing Wireless Communication Networks. She received her MA Sc in Electrical Engineering from Concordia, with the specialty in Wireless Telecommunications. She also holds a BSc in Electronic Engineering from the Isfahan University of Technology, Iran.Director – Monique Moumblow
